Christina's Charts and Graphs Experiment

Thanks for offering to help me out with this project! You will be shown 60 charts/graphs, and for each chart/graph you will be asked to compare the sizes of two data points in the graph. The data points are either marked with an asterisk (*) or they are colored in with the color grey (The page will tell you which to look for).

Please complete all 60 trials (I can't use the data otherwise), and once you're done you'll be brought to a screen that tells you how you did!
